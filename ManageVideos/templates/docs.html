Introduction

Our website provides a user-friendly platform for users to upload videos and enhance their content by adding captions and subtitles. Whether you have pre-existing caption files or want to create subtitles from scratch, our platform offers you the tools to ensure your videos are accessible and engaging to a wider audience.

Getting Started

Creating an Account

To begin using our platform, you'll need to create an account. Click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and provide the required information, including your email address and a secure password.

Logging In

Once you have an account, you can log in by clicking the "Log In" button and entering your credentials. If you forget your password, there is an option to reset it.

Uploading Videos

Supported Formats

Our platform supports a variety of video formats, including MP4, AVI, MKV, and more. Videos should be of good quality to ensure the best viewing experience.

Video Upload Process

  1. After logging in, click on the "Upload Video" button on your dashboard.
  2. Select the video file you wish to upload from your device.
  3. Provide essential details such as the video title, description, and category.
  4. Click "Upload" to initiate the upload process. Depending on the file size, this may take some time.
